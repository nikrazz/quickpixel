Local SEO: Simple Strategies to Boost Your Online Visibility

  • 17 September 2023
  • Nikolai

Introduction

If you’ve ever wondered why your high street bakery isn’t topping Google when someone searches “fresh croissants near me,” then you’re in the right place.

Let’s dive deep into the world of local SEO and put your business on the digital map!

What is local SEO?

Local SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is like your digital sat-nav guiding locals right to your doorstep. It’s all about promoting your services to local customers at the exact time they’re looking for them.

Imagine being the first name that pops up when someone’s hankering for a cappuccino in your town.

Yep, that’s the power of local SEO.

Local SEO vs. SEO

Alright, let’s break this down:

  • SEO is the general tweak-and-tune magic that boosts your site’s visibility on a national or even global scale. Picture it as fishing in the big ocean with a big net, hoping for a catch.

  • Local SEO, on the other hand, is more precise. It’s like fishing in a familiar pond using your granddad’s secret bait. You’re targeting the fish (read: customers) in your area because you know they’re there and they’re hungry!

Why local SEO is important

In this digital age, it’s not just about being online; it’s about being found.

Let’s hit you with a stat: 97% of people learn more about a local company online than anywhere else. If you’re not topping those local searches, you might as well be a ghost bakery – and no one orders from ghost bakeries!

Local SEO Audit

Before jumping in, it’s crucial to know where you stand. Here’s how:

  • Keyword research for local queries: What are folks in your area typing into Google? “Best vegan cupcakes in [Your Suburb]” perhaps?

  • Google Business Profile check: Ensure everything from your address to your opening hours is spot on.

  • Local reviews: If you’ve got a heap of 5-star reviews, fab! If not, it might be time to encourage some feedback.

  • Website optimisation: Slow sites lose customers. Is yours zippy enough?

  • Backlinks: Quality local backlinks can boost your credibility in the eyes of Google.

  • Local directories: Are you on them? You should be.

  • Schema check: A bit techy, but vital. Ensure localised schema is in place.

  • Internal linking: Keep it logical. Make it easy for visitors to hop from one relevant page to the next.

How do you improve your local SEO?

Here’s where we roll up our sleeves.

Understand Local SEO ranking factors

To conquer local SEO, you’ve got to get the lay of the land. Here’s what matters:

  • Location: Google loves relevance. If you’re nearby and relevant, you’re gold.

  • Google Business Profile listing: Keep it accurate, updated, and active.

  • Reviews and ratings: A good reputation goes a long way.

  • Content: Keep it local, keep it fresh.

  • NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency: This needs to be uniform everywhere, from your website to local directories.

  • Quality backlinks: Think of them as the community vouching for you.

  • Localised website: Does your site shout ‘local’? If not, time for a rethink.

Have a Local SEO strategy

Random efforts can lead to random results. Structure your approach:

  • Zero in on your unique offering: Maybe it’s those vegan cupcakes or perhaps the gluten-free pies?

  • Conduct a thorough SEO audit: Use the checklist we discussed.

  • Keyword research is king: Find out what the locals are searching for and then be the answer to their queries.

  • On-page SEO: Make sure each page of your site is a shining star.

  • Link building: This isn’t about spamming. It’s about building genuine local connections.

Create Locally Targeted Content

Lastly, think local. Write about the town fair. Review the new park. Discuss local happenings or how your products fit into the local culture. When your content resonates with the community, they’re more likely to resonate with you.

Wrap Up

In a nutshell, local SEO is about being the big fish in your pond. You might not be able to compete with the global giants, but in your neighbourhood, you can be the legend.

So, arm yourself with these strategies, and let’s make sure the next time someone searches for the best croissants in town, they’re heading straight to your bakery!
